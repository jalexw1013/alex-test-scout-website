About Us

We are a family-focused Cub Scout Pack in Pineville, NC. We meet on Mondays at 6:30 PM at Pineville
Church of the Nazarene. We welcome all kids from Kindergarten to 5th grade. Our goal is to help young
people make good choices by teaching the values of the Scout Oath and Scout Law

Why Join Us?

  • Bike rides, hikes, and seasonal celebrations
  • Two campouts per year, including an overnight on the Charlotte Knights outfield.
  • Pinewood Derby race event.
  • Community service projects.
  • Special scout nights with Charlotte sports teams.

Registration Information

  • Dues: $135 per child (full program cost is $260, offset by fundraising efforts that every family
    helps with!).
  • Want to Be A Den Leader? Great! Fill out the contact form and let us know
  • Registration method: Paper Application Only — submit your application directly to us. Pack 007
    pays Council dues and will send you an invoice for your portion (reduced for any volunteer
    discounts     you earn)

    After you reach out via the contact below, you can submit to this form to us via email.

Annual Calendar Highlights

Our year is filled with exciting, meaningful activities for every scout. Some of our major events include:

  • Bike rides, outdoor hikes, and more adventure!
    Pack meetings are held on Mondays, 6:30pm-7:30pm. We break for major holidays, spring, and summer
    vacations, focusing on special activities when school is out
  • Bobcat Ceremonies—celebrating every new scout’s first achievement
  • National Night Out with Pineville PD
  • King’s Mountain Hike (autumn adventure!)
  • Pineville Tree Lighting—help light up the holidays
  • Scouting for Food—community service project benefitting local families
  • STEM-Focused Activities—explore science, technology, engineering, and math in fun, hands-on
    ways
  • Potluck & Adult Awards—celebrate our successes as a community
  • Pinewood Derby—race day excitement for the whole pack
  • Sporting Team Scout Nights—exclusive access and fun in Charlotte

For a full calendar and our outside of meeting activities, checkout the calendar.

Family Involvement

Family is at the heart of Cub Scouting! We thrive because of our parents and guardians.

  • Our leaders are here to support and guide, but every parent can make a difference by assisting
    Den Leaders or joining our Pack Committee
  • You’re encouraged to attend meetings and help plan events—your involvement makes our pack
    stronger.
  • The more you volunteer, the more discounted dues you can earn!
  • Cub Scouting is a partnership—let’s create a great experience for your scout before worrying
    about paperwork!
